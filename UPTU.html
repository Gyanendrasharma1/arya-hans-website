UPTU / GBTU Program

This program is specifically designed for students preparing for the UPTU (AKTU) entrance examination. It emphasizes building a solid foundation in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics while equipping students with the skills required to perform effectively in a competitive exam environment.

  • Experienced faculty with strong expertise in state-level competitive examinations
  • Structured and concise study material focused on exam relevance
  • Regular mock tests designed to simulate the actual exam pattern
  • Chapter-wise and full syllabus test series
  • Dedicated doubt-clearing sessions for personalized support
  • Performance analysis with detailed feedback for continuous improvement
  • Complete syllabus coverage aligned with the latest AKTU exam pattern
  • Intensive problem-solving sessions to boost speed and accuracy
  • Previous year questions (PYQs) with detailed solution approach
  • Rank-oriented preparation strategy for top performance
  • Time management techniques for maximizing exam efficiency
  • Students who have completed or are currently studying in Class 12 with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics (PCM)
  • Candidates aspiring to gain admission into engineering or other professional programs through AKTU

Program duration ranges from 6 months to 1 year, based on the selected course

  • Regular Batch: Classes are conducted 5 days a week with daily sessions of 3–4 hours
  • Weekend Batch: Extended classes of around 6 hours per day on Saturdays and Sundays
  • Crash Course: Fast-paced 2–3 month program designed for intensive last-stage preparation
