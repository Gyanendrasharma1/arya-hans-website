NEET Syllabus Overview

The NEET examination is a crucial step for students aiming to pursue undergraduate medical courses such as MBBS and BDS across India. It evaluates a student's conceptual understanding and application skills in core science subjects.

The syllabus is structured around topics from Classes 11 and 12, covering Physics, Chemistry, and Biology. A strong grip on these fundamentals is essential for achieving a high score and securing admission into top medical colleges.
