JEE Main Syllabus Overview

The JEE Main examination is one of the most important entrance tests for students aiming to pursue engineering programs in top institutes across India. It evaluates a student’s understanding of core subjects including Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics.

The syllabus is based on concepts from Classes 11 and 12, focusing on conceptual clarity, application, and problem-solving ability. A structured understanding of the syllabus helps students plan their preparation effectively and concentrate on high-weightage topics.

At Aryahans Institute, we guide students with a strategic approach, expert mentorship, and consistent practice to ensure complete syllabus coverage and strong exam performance.
