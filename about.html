About Aryahans Education Institute

About Aryahans Education Institute

Aryahans Educational Institute Pvt. Ltd., established in 1995, is a premier coaching institute dedicated to preparing students for IIT-JEE and other competitive engineering entrance examinations. Over the years, the institute has expanded its academic offerings to include Medical Entrance (NEET), NTSE, Olympiads, NDA, and CDS preparation.

Our Academic Excellence

Since its inception, Aryahans has consistently delivered outstanding results, building a strong reputation not only across Uttar Pradesh but throughout India. This success is driven by a well-structured academic approach, continuous planning, and a strong focus on student satisfaction through regular feedback and improvement.

The institute has successfully guided numerous students into IITs and other prestigious engineering colleges. Many students have also excelled in NTSE, science and mathematics Olympiads, and defense entrance examinations like NDA.

At Aryahans, we believe that engineering is more than just a career—it is a responsibility to contribute to nation-building. Our programs are carefully designed to provide in-depth coaching based on the latest syllabus and exam patterns of IIT-JEE and other competitive examinations.

Our Teaching Approach

We follow a systematic and student-focused methodology to ensure consistent academic success.
