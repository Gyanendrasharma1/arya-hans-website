NEET UG

Aryahans Educational Institute Pvt. Ltd., our NEET UG program is thoughtfully structured for aspiring medical students who aim to achieve admission into premier medical colleges across India. With a strong emphasis on Physics, Chemistry, and Biology, the course is designed to build a solid conceptual foundation, enhance analytical thinking, and develop exam-oriented strategies.

  • Learn under the guidance of highly experienced and dedicated faculty specializing in medical entrance preparation.
  • Well-structured, easy-to-understand study material designed with a strong focus on NEET exam patterns and concepts.
  • Regular tests and performance evaluations to monitor progress and highlight areas for improvement.
  • Interactive classroom environment that encourages active participation and instant doubt clarification.
  • Personalized feedback and detailed performance analysis to help every student improve consistently.
  • Dedicated doubt-solving sessions (online + offline)
  • Complete and structured coverage of the NEET UG syllabus in alignment with the latest exam pattern and guidelines.
  • Frequent topic-wise tests along with full-length mock exams to strengthen preparation and improve accuracy.
  • Strong focus on Biology supported by equal attention to Physics and Chemistry for balanced performance.
  • Individual mentoring and academic guidance provided by experienced faculty to support each student’s learning journey.
  • Time management & exam strategy sessions
  • Students of Class 11 & 12 (Science stream)
  • Aspirants who are determined to pursue a career in medicine, healthcare, or other related professional fields.
  • Students targeting top NEET ranks

Flexible course duration ranging from 1 to 2 years, designed to match the academic level and preparation needs of students.

  • Regular Classroom Program: Conducted 5 days a week with 3–4 hours of focused academic sessions each day.
  • Weekend Program: Extended learning sessions of approximately 6 hours conducted on Saturdays and Sundays for added flexibility.
  • Crash Course Program: Intensive daily classes over a short duration of 2–3 months, designed for rapid revision and exam readiness.
