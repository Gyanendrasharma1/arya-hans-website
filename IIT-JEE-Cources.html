IIT JEE Advanced Program

Designed for aspirants targeting top ranks in IIT JEE Advanced. This program focuses on deep conceptual clarity, advanced problem-solving, and strategic exam preparation for top IIT selections.

  • Mentorship by IIT alumni and experienced faculty
  • Advanced level study material with concept depth
  • Daily problem practice sheets (DPP)
  • Chapter-wise and full syllabus test series
  • Detailed performance analytics and rank tracking
  • Dedicated doubt-solving sessions (online + offline)
  • Complete coverage of Physics, Chemistry & Mathematics
  • High-level problem-solving sessions
  • Previous year questions (PYQ) focused training
  • Personal mentorship for rank improvement
  • Time management & exam strategy sessions
  • Students of Class 11 & 12 (Science stream)
  • Droppers preparing for IIT JEE Advanced
  • Students targeting top IIT ranks

Program duration ranges from 1 to 2 years depending on batch selection. Crash courses of 2–3 months are also available for revision.

  • Regular Batch: 5 days/week (3–4 hours/day)
  • Weekend Batch: 6–7 hours (Saturday & Sunday)
  • Crash Course: Daily intensive sessions (2–3 months)

IIT JEE Main Program

The JEE Main program at RS Institute is strategically designed to build strong conceptual foundations in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics. It helps students develop speed, accuracy, and exam confidence required to secure top ranks and admissions into leading NITs, IIITs, and other engineering colleges.

  • Guidance from experienced faculty and IIT-qualified mentors
  • Structured study material with concept clarity and practice sets
  • Flexible learning modes for better student convenience
  • Regular mock tests designed to match real exam patterns
  • Performance analysis with detailed feedback and improvement tracking
  • Complete syllabus coverage aligned with JEE Main pattern
  • Topic-wise tests along with full-length mock exams
  • Focused training on speed, accuracy, and time management
  • Personalized mentorship for academic growth and strategy
  • Students currently studying in Class 11 or 12
  • Students who have completed Class 12
  • Aspirants aiming for JEE Main and engineering admissions

The program duration ranges from 1 to 2 years depending on the selected course. Short-term crash courses are also available for revision and final preparation.

  • Regular Batch: 5 days a week (3–4 hours per day)
  • Weekend Batch: Extended sessions on Saturdays and Sundays
  • Crash Course: Intensive daily classes for 2–3 months
