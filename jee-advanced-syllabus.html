JEE Advanced Syllabus Overview

The JEE Advanced examination is one of the most prestigious and challenging engineering entrance exams in India. It serves as the gateway to the Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs) and evaluates students on deep conceptual clarity and analytical thinking.

The syllabus is derived from Classes 11 and 12, covering Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics with a strong focus on advanced problem-solving and multi-concept application.
