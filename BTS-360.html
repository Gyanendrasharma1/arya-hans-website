BTS 360 – Biology Crash Course

Overview

A comprehensive crash program designed for NEET aspirants to complete the entire Biology syllabus through intensive, topic-wise sessions. The course ensures strong conceptual clarity along with consistent revision and test practice.

Course Structure

  • Complete coverage of Zoology and Botany in a systematic sequence
  • Daily topic-wise sessions for focused learning
  • Parallel progression of both subjects for balanced preparation
  • Multiple revisions integrated within the schedule

What Students Get

  • Detailed explanation of every NCERT concept
  • Repeated practice of high-weightage topics
  • Chapter-wise test discussions
  • Previous year NEET questions with solutions
  • Quick revision modules before tests

Test System

  • Weekly full-length and chapter-based tests
  • NCERT-focused assessments for accuracy improvement
  • Real exam-level question patterns
  • Performance analysis with improvement strategy

Duration & Format

  • Intensive 2–3 month crash program
  • Daily classes with structured timetable
  • Designed for last-stage preparation and revision

Why BTS 360

  • Covers entire Biology syllabus in a limited time
  • Focus on NCERT + exam-oriented approach
  • Ideal for students aiming for rapid improvement
  • Balanced focus on concept + practice + revision
