BTS 360 – Biology Crash Course

Fast-track your NEET Biology preparation with a high-impact crash program designed for maximum retention, revision, and exam performance.

Program Overview

BTS 360 is an intensive Biology crash course focused on complete syllabus coverage, NCERT mastery, and exam-oriented preparation. The program is ideal for students aiming to boost scores in a short time through structured learning and testing.

Key Features

  • Complete Zoology & Botany coverage in structured sequence
  • NCERT line-by-line explanation with concept clarity
  • High-weightage topic focus for score maximization
  • Daily practice + revision integration
  • Previous Year Questions (PYQ) discussion

Learning Approach

  • Concept → Practice → Revision model
  • Short, focused topic-wise sessions
  • Parallel subject coverage (Botany + Zoology)
  • Continuous doubt-solving support

Testing & Evaluation

  • Weekly full-length mock tests
  • Chapter-wise assessments
  • NCERT-based accuracy improvement tests
  • Detailed performance analysis & feedback

Course Details

  • Duration: 2–3 Months Intensive
  • Mode: Online + Offline Available
  • Schedule: Daily Structured Classes
  • Target: NEET 2026 / 2027 Aspirants

Why Choose BTS 360?

  • Fast syllabus completion without compromising clarity
  • Strong focus on NCERT (NEET core)
  • Perfect for last-stage preparation
  • Balanced approach: Concept + Practice + Tests
